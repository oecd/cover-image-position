This cover simulator allows you to get an idea how an illustration would look on a standard 210 x 280 mm publication cover.
You may use it for reference, but the image will still need to conform to PAC's requirements for cover illustrations.
High quality images (with a minimal resolution of 300 dpi) are recommended for optimal print quality and for any follow-up digital products.
Licenses to use images need to be provided when the cover process starts.
Please also read: "Evolution of the Publication Cover" on the OECD Authors Community.
